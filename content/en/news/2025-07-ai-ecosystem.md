Under the guidance of the [**Ministry of Digital Affairs (MODA)**](https://moda.gov.tw/en/) for the "AI Industry Ecosystem and Multi-Talent Empowerment Program," Twinkle AI was invited to lead a technical session. We joined partners across industry, government, and academia to share our fine-tuning insights and practical development experiences, exploring ways to build a more resilient and complete local AI technical ecosystem.

#### Time and Location
* **Event Date:** 2025/07/30 (Wed)
* **Event Time:** 13:30 ~ 16:00
* **Location:** No. 287, Sec. 3, Chengde Rd., Datong Dist., Taipei City
