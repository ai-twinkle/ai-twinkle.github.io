The Twinkle AI team officially unveiled **Formosa Vision**, a project designed to address the cultural blind spots of global Vision Language Models (VLMs). We curated thousands of image-text pairs from the **[National Cultural Memory Bank](https://tcmb.culture.tw/)**, applying precise Data Cleaning and linguistic calibration to ensure AI can accurately recognize unique Taiwanese landscapes—from historic trails and military housing to the battlefields of Matsu.
